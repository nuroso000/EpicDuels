## For Players

### Main Features
- 🎮 **1v1 Duels** — Challenge other players directly
- 📊 **Stats Tracking** — Track your wins and losses
- 🎯 **Matchmaking Queue** — Automatic matching with other players
- 🛠️ **Kit System** — Different equipment sets for different modes
- 🏆 **Multiple Arenas** — Play on different maps

👉 **[More in Player Guide →](./03-Player-Guide.md)**
